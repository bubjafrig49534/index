bminus was a bicycle apparel company.

bminus was founded by
marc hummel + leah cipolla in 2004, with a simple idea: a tee-shirt with two people on a tandem above the words "bicycles are for lovers".

hundreds of tee-shirts later, bminus mailed its last hand-packed parcel in 2011. reminisce with us, won't you?
